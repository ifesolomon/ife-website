Vehicle Buying Guide

Trade In Your Vehicle

Learn how trading in your current vehicle can reduce the cost of your next purchase and simplify the buying process.

← Back to Vehicle Buying Guides

Trading in your current vehicle is one of the easiest ways to reduce the cost of your next purchase. Instead of selling privately, you can apply its value directly toward your next vehicle.

Reduce the Cost of Your Next Vehicle

The value of your trade in can be used as part of your down payment, reducing the amount you need to finance and, in many cases, lowering your monthly payments.

Save Time and Avoid the Hassle

Selling privately can involve advertising, answering enquiries, scheduling viewings, negotiating with buyers, and completing paperwork. Trading in your vehicle keeps the process simpler and more convenient.

One Simple Transaction

When you trade in your current vehicle and purchase your next one at the same dealership, the appraisal, purchase, financing, and delivery can all be handled in one place.

Receive a Fair Market Evaluation

Your trade in value is usually based on factors such as year, model, mileage, condition, vehicle history, and current market demand.

Prepare Your Vehicle for the Best Value

Before bringing your vehicle in, wash and clean it, remove personal belongings, bring all keys and accessories, and have maintenance records available if possible.

My Advice

If you are thinking about replacing your vehicle, I recommend getting a trade in appraisal before making your decision. Knowing your current vehicle's value can help you understand your budget and explore your options with confidence.
